Check out some of the case studies I made as a Data Analyst.

Financial KPI Dashboard

This Financial Dashboard brings together key metrics—such as current sales, taxes, assets, and liabilities—into one unified view, allowing for quick comparisons against last month’s figures. The top cards highlight each indicator’s value, past performance, and percentage changes, while the charts below reveal actual and cumulative trends over multiple years. Switching between tabs offers a closer look at assets, liabilities, sales, and taxes, making it easy to pinpoint where the business is gaining or losing momentum. Overall, the layout is designed to provide an at-a-glance summary of financial health and encourage data-driven decisions.

Project Health Scorecard

This Project gathers key metrics—like project count, budget, expenses, and average duration—into a single dashboard to track the status and progress of various initiatives. A map displays project locations, while bar and pie charts break down projects by type, priority, and completion status. Users can see how budgets compare to actual expenses for each project manager, and a detailed table offers deeper insights into start/end dates and percentage completion. Overall, it provides a quick, visual way to monitor project performance and spot potential issues early.

Foreign Direct Investment in various sectors of India

This FDI (Foreign Direct Investment) Dashboard provides a clear snapshot of how different sectors attract or fall behind in foreign investment. It highlights the top ten and bottom ten performers, shows how FDI has varied over the years, and includes a clustering analysis that groups similar sectors. Users can also view a forecast predicting future trends based on historical data. Overall, the layout is designed to spotlight emerging growth areas and potential gaps in foreign investment flows.

Interactive Healthcare Dashboard

This Heart Failure Survival Analysis Dashboard showcases how different patient attributes—such as smoking status, age, diabetes, and serum creatinine levels—correlate with survival outcomes. Each visual is designed to highlight patterns, including which factors appear most influential. Users can quickly see how subgroups differ, whether by age-sex combination or ejection fraction range. The layout helps pinpoint potential risks and opportunities for intervention, all in one concise view.

Netflix Usage dashboard

This Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard highlights how thousands of movies and TV shows are distributed across genres, ratings, and countries. The map pinpoints where each title originates, while bar and bubble charts reveal category splits (like “Movie” vs. “TV Show”), top genres, and rating types. Year-by-year trends also illustrate Netflix’s growing library and user preferences over time. Overall, it offers a quick visual overview of how Netflix content is spread out and how it evolves.

Amazon Prime Video Usage

This Amazon Prime Video Content Analysis Dashboard consolidates key information about movies and TV shows available on the platform—spanning genres, ratings, release years, and countries of origin. An interactive map reveals which regions contribute the most titles, and users can quickly filter by genre or rating to see how content is distributed. The dashboard also compares the share of movies versus TV shows, spotlighting overall growth and user preferences. Ultimately, it provides a concise visual snapshot of Prime Video’s library composition and evolution.
